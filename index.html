The International 2018 (TI8) is the ongoing eighth iteration of The International, an annual Dota 2 eSports championship tournament. Hosted by Valve Corporation, the game's developer, TI8 followed a year-long series of tournaments awarding qualifying points, known as the Dota Pro Circuit (DPC), with the top eight ranking teams being directly invited to the tournament. In addition, ten more teams earned invites through qualifiers that were held in June 2018, with the main event to be played at the Rogers Arena in Vancouver in August. As with every International, the prize pool was crowdfunded by the Dota 2 community via its battle pass feature, with the total being over US$24 million.
